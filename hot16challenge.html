#hot16challenge is a Polish nationwide music campaign launched in 2014. According to its rules, each artist must record a 16-bar song within 72 hours of receiving a nomination and nominate other artists for the same task. The original version of the beat used in the song was unfinished and unmixed; also, it had been published under the old name – Krasnal.

Following the success of the single "Zrobiłem Ją", released in summer 2014 on the Prosto Label as part of the debut album "Wiry", a new track was created together with Żyto for the #hot16challenge.

Instrumental released by DAOrecords in June 2021.
